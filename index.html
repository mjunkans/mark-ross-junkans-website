About Mark

Mark Ross Junkans is an author exploring the intersection of faith, technology, and modern life. His work examines how we can maintain authentic spiritual connection in an increasingly digital world. Through books like "The Return of Mystery" and the "3 Verses a Day" devotional series, Mark helps readers navigate the challenges of faith in contemporary society while building lasting spiritual habits.
